About Us

Our Story

The Association "Jasna Idnina" was founded in Nebregovo, Dolneni, Prilep in December 2008 by a group of young people. The activities which Jasna Idnina implements have a national and international character, the majority of them being international. The members of Jasna Idnina put their efforts into the development of a sustainable structure that will be able to support, assist and provide consulting and education services to young people for a long period of time, in a way to become a regional youth center in order to meet the association's main objectives.

Our Mission

Non-formal education is an important part of the association's work, a tool which we use on a regular basis to satisfy the educational needs of local young people from rural areas (which comprise our main target group) and to support and develop human capacities in rural areas. The main aim of our activities is to provide learning and employment opportunities to young people from rural areas, a lot of them having fewer opportunities due to geographical, educational and economic obstacles. Jasna Idnina's target group is young people that are between the ages of 15 to 30, from Nebregovo and the other rural areas around Prilep, as well as the city of Prilep. The association strives to promote lifelong learning among other things, and that is why it works on activities which would include the general population of rural and remote areas.

Jasna Indina's Goals

Goal 1

Bringing young people together on a basis of equity, regardless of their national, religious, educational, social or economic belonging.

Goal 2

Affirmation of young people through their individual capabilities and uniqueness.

Goal 3

Bringing young people together to increase their mutual cooperation and joint work in the future.

Goal 4

Providing educational opportunities for young people, and adult learners too, to develop entrepreneurship among them.

Goal 5

Nurturing creativity and culture, as well as using creativity and art as a means for alternative thinking in modern industries.

Jasna Idnina's Main Objectives

Objective 1

Organization of seminars, lectures, sports events, concerts, competitions for the purpose of a better introduction to young people's cultures.

Objective 2

Assisting and mediation between young people by using their distinctiveness and skills.

Objective 3

Supporting international youth exchanges of high schoolers, as well as winter and summer youth exchanges of young people to become better acquainted with the culture and traditions of other peoples.
